Our Legacy

  • AFX was founded. August 2011

    AFX Dance is a hip hop/urban dance group founded by Ping Quach, Geoffrey Chen, and Rosemary Hua in Fall 2011 to give dancers an opportunity to grow. Our name, [A]spirations, [F]amily, e[X]perience reflects our philosophy of creating a family environment for dancers to build up the confidence and support to reach new heights.

  • We had our 1 millionth boba social. Dec 2014

    The primary activity that our dance teams do outside of practice is drink boba, go to Shooting Star to talk about our favorite flavors of boba, or take baths in pools of boba.

  • We performed in front of Obama. July 2016

    Our biggest honor was being able to go to Washington D.C. and dab in front of the president of the United States.
